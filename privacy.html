Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2, 2026

Introduction

At ARISE Platform ("we," "our," or "us"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you order products from our website, specifically the Ramadan Journal.

Information We Collect

When you place an order for the Ramadan Journal, we collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: First name, last name, email address, phone number
  • Shipping Address: Street address, city, state/province, postal code, country
  • Order Information: Products ordered, order ID, timestamp
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information (for security and analytics)

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Order Fulfillment: To process and ship your Ramadan Journal order
  • Communication: To send order confirmations, shipping updates, and delivery notifications
  • Customer Support: To respond to inquiries and resolve issues
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

We will NOT:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Use your data for marketing without your explicit consent
  • Share your information except as required for order fulfillment (e.g., shipping carriers)

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We may share your information with:

  • Shipping Carriers: To deliver your order (e.g., Pakistan Post, courier services)
  • Payment Processors: If using bank transfer or online payment (data is encrypted and secure)
  • Email Service Providers: To send transactional emails (e.g., SendGrid, Mailgun)

These third parties are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the specified purposes.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Secure storage with access controls
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Anti-spam measures (honeypot, reCAPTCHA)

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but strive to protect your data using best practices.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy:

  • Order Data: Retained for 3 years for accounting and legal compliance
  • Shipping Data: Retained for 1 year after delivery
  • Email Communications: Retained as long as you maintain an active relationship with us

You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Your Rights below).

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service

To exercise these rights, contact us at: privacy@arise-platform.com

Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses minimal cookies and tracking:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for website functionality (e.g., form submission)
  • Analytics: Google Analytics (anonymized IP) to understand visitor behavior

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect website functionality.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Sending an email notification (for material changes)

Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
