SISTER PAGE PLACEHOLDER TEMPLATE
=================================

This template is used for all sister pages until content is finalized.

[Name] - Sister's full name
[Title & Role] - Professional title and role
[Badge 1/2/3] - Hero section badges
[Specialization 1-4] - Areas of expertise
[Service 1-4] - Services offered
[Credential 1-4] - Qualifications and credentials
[Stat 1-3] - Quick statistics
[Phone Number] - Contact phone
[Email] - Contact email
[Day Range/Day] - Availability schedule
[Time] - Available hours
[About content to be added] - Biography paragraph
[Description] - Service/specialization description
[Duration & Pricing] - Service details
[#] - Numeric stat value

All content is marked with [] brackets for easy identification.
